1. SECTION 1

\(\boldsymbol{1.1}\)
If \(32\sin^{6}\theta = 10 - 15 \cos2\theta + b \cos4\theta + a\cos6\theta\) show that \(\tan\theta\tan5\theta = \frac{a + b - 5}{a - b + 7}\)
We know \[ \begin{align} \cos2\theta &= \cos\theta\cos\theta - \sin\theta\sin\theta \\ &= \cos^{2}\theta - \sin^{2}\theta \\ &= 1 - \sin^{2}\theta - \sin^{2}\theta \\ &= 1 - 2\sin^{2}\theta \\ \Rightarrow2\sin^{2}\theta &= 1 - \cos2\theta \end{align} \] Using this we can write \[ \begin{align} 32\sin^{6}\theta &= 4\cdot8\sin^{6}\theta \\ &= 4(2\sin^{2}\theta)^{3} \\ &= 4(1 - \cos2\theta)^{3} \\ &= 4(1^3 - \cos^{3}2\theta - \ 3\cos2\theta + 3\cos^{2}2\theta) \\ &= 4 - 12\cos2\theta - \ 4\cos^{3}2\theta + \ 12\cos^{2}2\theta \tag{1}\label{eq:1.1.1} \end{align} \] Letting \(A = 2\theta\) we can write 3rd term equation (\ref{eq:1.1.1})'s right side as \[ \begin{align} 4\cos^{3}2\theta &= 4\cos^{3}A \\ &= \cos3A + 3\cos A \ \qquad\left[ \because \cos3A = \ 4\cos^{3}A - 3\cos A\right] \\ \Rightarrow 4\cos^{3}2\theta &= \cos6\theta + 3\cos2\theta \ \tag{2}\label{eq:1.1.2} \end{align} \] Likewise we can write \[ \begin{align} 12\cos^{2}\theta &= 6 \cdot 2\cos^{2}\theta \\ &= 6 \cos 2\cos^{2}A \\ &= 6(\cos 2A + 1) \\ &= 6\cos 4\theta + 6 \tag{3}\label{eq:1.1.3} \end{align} \] Using equations (\ref{eq:1.1.2}) and (\ref{eq:1.1.3}) in equation (\ref{eq:1.1.1}) we get, \[ \begin{align} 32\sin^{6}\theta &= 4 - 12\cos 2\theta - \cos 6\theta \ - 3\cos 2\theta + 6 \cos 4\theta + 6 \\ &= 10 - 15 \cos 2\theta + 6 \cos 4\theta \ - \cos 6\theta \tag{4}\label{eq:1.1.4} \end{align} \] Using the given information \(32\sin^{6}\theta = 10 - 15 \cos2\theta + b \cos4\theta + a\cos6\theta\) in euqation(\ref{eq:1.1.4}) we get: \[ \begin{align} 10 - 15 \cos 2\theta + 6 \cos 4\theta - \cos 6\theta \ &= 10 - 15 \cos2\theta + b \cos4\theta + a\cos6\theta \\ \Rightarrow (6 - b)\cos4\theta &= (1 + a)\cos 6\theta \\ \Rightarrow \frac{\cos 4\theta}{\cos 6\theta} \ &= \frac{1 + a}{6 - b} \\ \Rightarrow \frac{\cos 4\theta - \cos 6\theta}{\cos 4\theta + \cos 6\theta} \ &= \frac{1 + a - 6 + b}{1 + a + 6 - b} \ \qquad \left[ \because a/b = c/d \Rightarrow \frac{a-b}{a+b}=\frac{c-d}{c+d}\right] \\ \Rightarrow \frac{\cos(5\theta - \theta) - \cos(5\theta + \theta)}{\cos(5\theta - \theta) + \cos(5\theta + \theta)}\ &= \frac{a+b-5}{a-b+7} \\ \Rightarrow \frac{2\sin 5\theta\sin \theta}{2\cos5\theta\cos \theta}\ &= \frac{a+b-5}{a-b+7} \\ \Rightarrow \tan \theta\tan 5\theta \ &= \frac{a+b-5}{a-b+7} \end{align} \] Hence proved.
